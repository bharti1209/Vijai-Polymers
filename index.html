WHO WE ARE

A trusted name in plastic manufacturing since 1993.

For over 25 years, Vijai Polymers has been manufacturing high-quality LDPE and LLDPE sheets for businesses across India. What began as a family-owned manufacturing enterprise has grown into a trusted partner for infrastructure companies, industrial manufacturers, agricultural businesses, distributors, and government organizations.

Our focus has remained the same from day one: manufacture reliable products, maintain consistent quality, and build long-term customer relationships.

With an emphasis on precision, transparency, and continuous improvement, we help our customers source polymer solutions they can depend on.
